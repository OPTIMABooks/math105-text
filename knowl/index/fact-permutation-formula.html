Fact 3.4.7.

A permutation of n items taken r at a time is used to count the number of ways to arrange (or permute) \(r\) items chosen from \(n\) possible items. The formula for this permutation is:
\begin{equation*} P(n,r) = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}\text{.} \end{equation*}
