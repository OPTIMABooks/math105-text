Definition 4.4.8.

The sample standard deviation, \(s\) is the average distance of a data point from the mean of a data set. That is,
\begin{equation*} s=\sqrt{\frac{\text{sum of the squared deviations from the mean}}{n-1}}\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(n\) is the sample size.
