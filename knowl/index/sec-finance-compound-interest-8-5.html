Effective Rate Formula.

The effective interest rate or annual percentage yield is given by
\begin{equation*} r_{\text{eff}} = \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^n -1, \end{equation*}
where \(r_{\text{eff}}\) is the effective interest rate, \(r\) is the nominal interest rate per year, and \(n\) is the number of compounding periods per year.
