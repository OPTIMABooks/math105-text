Fact 2.3.7. Logical Equivalence of the Contrapositive.

A conditional statement and its contrapositive are equivalent. The converse and inverse of a conditional statement are also equivalent. That is,
\begin{align*} p \to q \equiv\amp \neg q\to \neg p \\ q \to p \equiv\amp \neg p\to \neq q \end{align*}
However, \(p\to q\) is NOT equivalent to \(q\to p\text{.}\)
